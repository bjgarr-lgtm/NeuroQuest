SootheBirb v2.5.0p — Router Rescue + Add‑ons
--------------------------------------------
Drop-in files (keep your index.html and styles.css):
  - app.js   (replace your root app.js)
  - assets/acc/{crown.svg, glasses.svg, cape.svg, torch.svg}
  - assets/sfx/{ding.wav, coin.wav, level.wav, bgm.wav}

This version forces hash-based navigation to show the correct view even
if your original router is missing/disabled. It detects views by:
  #route-id, [data-view="route"], [data-screen="route"], [data-route-target="route"]
Fallback route: 'home' or the first detected section.

After deploy:
  1) Purge Cloudflare Pages cache.
  2) Hard-refresh (Cmd/Ctrl-Shift-R).
  3) Navigate via top nav or tiles; views should swap without 'Not found'.
